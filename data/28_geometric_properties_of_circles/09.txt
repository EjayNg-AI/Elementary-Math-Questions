$A, B, C, D$ and $E$ are points on the circle, with centre $O$. The diameter of the circle, $B E$, is parallel to $C D$. Angle $O A C=40^{\circ}$ and angle $A C B=35^{\circ}$. $D E F$ is a straight line. Find, giving reasons for each answer,

(a) $\angle O E A$,

(b) reflex $\angle A O B$,

(c) $\angle B A C$,

(d) $\angle F E B$,

(e) $\angle D O E$.
