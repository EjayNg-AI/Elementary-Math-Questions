The points $A, B, C, D$ and $E$ lie on a circle with centre $O$. $FG$ is a tangent to the circle at $C$. It is given that $\angle A E C=77^{\circ}$ and $\angle E A O=63^{\circ}$. Giving your reasons clearly, find

(a) $\angle A B C$,

(b) Obtuse $\angle A O C$,

(c) $\angle E C O$,

(d) reflex $\angle A C F$.
