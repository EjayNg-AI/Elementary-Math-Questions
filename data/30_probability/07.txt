There are 3 white balls and 2 green balls in a bag. A ball is drawn at random from the bag, the colour noted and the ball is not replaced. A second ball is then drawn.

(a) Draw the possibility diagram to show the outcome of the draw.

(b) Find, as a fraction in its simplest form, the probability that

(i) both balls are green,

(ii) at most 1 green ball

(iii) at least 1 green ball
